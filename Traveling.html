Traveling

I really like traveling, both shorter and longer trips. My biggest trip so far was in 2010 when I travelled for 6 months through South America. Here are some of my favorite photos from my travels.

This photo is taken on a cliff on a small island south of Iceland and we can see the south coast of Iceland and the glacier Eyjafjallajökull.

This one is also taken from the same cliff and you can see two small islands called Bjarnarey and Elliðaey, and in the back Eyjafjallajökull.

This photo is taken in Chile when my friends and I were volunteering for Techo para Chile, building houses for families that lost theirs during the earthquake in 2010. The children are from the family that we were building the house for.

This is the volcano Licancabur on the borders of Chile and Bolivia. In front of the the volcano is the Laguna Verde.

This is taken in Cuzco in Peru.

This photo is taken on an island outside of Cartagena in Colombia.

This is taken when my friends and I cycled down The Death Road in Bolivia. Behind us you can see a mudslide that is being cleared.
