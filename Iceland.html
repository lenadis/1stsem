Iceland

Like most Icelandic people I am proud of my country, the nature is beautiful and the people are friendly.

My favorite place in Iceland is Snæfellsnes peninsula. My grandmother grew up there right under Snæfellsjökull glacier, which was the entrance to the center of the earth in the famous book by Jules Verne. My family built a summer house there in 2002 so we can enjoy the beautiful surroundings all year around.

The white specs on the right is the old farm.

In the top right corner is Snæfellsjökull glacier.
